## Overview

Lux HR needed a digital presence that communicated professionalism while feeling warm and approachable — a balance essential for an HR consulting firm. The challenge was designing a website that builds trust quickly, clearly communicates services, and drives conversion.

## Approach

I started with stakeholder interviews to understand the brand values: trust, clarity, and human connection. From there, I developed a warm visual system using soft golds, cream tones, and clean typography that felt both professional and inviting.

## Key Design Decisions

- **Warm color palette** with soft golds and neutral tones to convey approachability
- **Clear information hierarchy** to guide visitors from services to contact
- **Trust-building elements** including testimonial sections and clear calls-to-action
- **Responsive layouts** optimized for both desktop and mobile consultations

## Outcome

The redesigned website improved clarity of service offerings and created a cohesive visual identity that resonated with the target audience of small-to-medium businesses seeking HR support.
