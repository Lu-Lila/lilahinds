## Overview

Salt Tampa Outreach is a nonprofit organization serving the Tampa Bay community. Their existing website struggled with unclear messaging, low engagement, and poor conversion on donation and volunteer sign-up pages. The redesign aimed to improve clarity, drive user action, and better represent their mission.

## Approach

I conducted a UX audit of the existing site, identifying pain points in navigation, content hierarchy, and calls-to-action. User interviews with volunteers and donors informed the redesign priorities.

## Key Design Decisions

- **Simplified navigation** reducing the main menu from 12 items to 5 clear categories
- **Impact-first storytelling** with community photos and outcome statistics above the fold
- **Streamlined donation flow** reducing steps from 5 to 3
- **Accessible design** with high-contrast text and screen-reader-friendly markup

## Outcome

The redesigned website saw improved clarity around the organization's mission and programs. The streamlined donation and volunteer sign-up flows created clearer pathways for user action and engagement.
