## Overview

Say Hello Neighbor is a community initiative that brings neighbors together through shared stories and local events. The project required building a complete brand identity from scratch — logo, color system, typography, and a website that embodied the spirit of neighborly connection.

## Approach

I immersed myself in the organization's mission and community stories to develop a brand identity that felt genuine and inviting. The visual language needed to feel like a warm invitation rather than a corporate announcement.

## Key Design Decisions

- **Hand-drawn logo elements** to convey warmth and authenticity
- **Earthy, welcoming color palette** inspired by front-porch gatherings
- **Story-first content layout** that centers community narratives
- **Photography guidelines** for consistent, candid community imagery

## Outcome

The brand identity and website successfully captured the spirit of community connection. The story-first approach resulted in higher engagement with community content and increased event participation.
